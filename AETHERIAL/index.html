FEATURES



HELM-LOCK

When the helmet is not worn, it ensures that the vehicle won't start, promoting responsible and safe riding habits.

LIFE-LINE

In the unfortunate event of a crash, AETHERIAL automatically sends real-time notifications to emergency contacts

SOS-ALERT

Users can easily trigger the SOS feature, which sends immediate alerts to pre-configured contacts and emergency services.

WE-TRAC

AETHERIAL leverages GPS technology to provide geolocation tracking in case of emergencies.

ECG SYSTEM(*)

Installed ECG to track heart-rate and send the live report to the ambulance when BP goes low.
